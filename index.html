Expert Review

In this project, we worked with James Madison University's library to run a usability test and assess the usability of their eBook resources. We wanted to see what preferences the students held and how they interacted with the different interfaces that James Madison University provided for them.

In order to do this, we had five students perform a variety of tasks in three separate eBook platforms. Prior to this, I evaluated the eBook platforms on my own according to Nielsen's heuristics and Peter Morville's Honeycomb heuristics.
